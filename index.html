Chris Lüscher, iA Zurich

iA creates digital products. We offer strategic design and consulting services through our UX studios in Zurich, Tokyo and Berlin.

Selected Projects

Wikipedia: Fundraising in Japan

In close collaboration with Wikipedia’s marketing and internal designers, we analyzed their existing strategies for fundraising in Japan, suggested new approaches with regards to strategy, design and copy, and accompanied them through the A/B testing of these ideas.

Vogue: Design Strategy

We worked with senior management, product managers and the UX team at Condé Nast International, guiding UX research in Japan, assisting in Germany and Italy, and offering new approaches of digital publishing in the times of Snapchat and Instagram.

iA Writer

The New York Times calls it “clever”, the Guardian finds it “beautiful and simple.” To us, iA Writer is the simple plain text editor we always wanted. In development since 2011, with more than a million regular users.

Studios

iA Zurich

iA Labs
